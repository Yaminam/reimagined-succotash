# Ammar - bottles + page images

Two jobs in this folder: **/bottles** (20 product packshots) and **/page-images** (all photography used across the 9 site pages). 'Location in your folder' = where the file sits inside Ammar/. 'Drop back to' = where the finished file goes in /public/images.

## How these images are required

**Bottles (bottles/):**
- Format: **PNG-24 with transparent alpha** - NO background, NO white box, NO drop shadow baked in.
- Full bottle in frame, upright, centred, label sharp and readable.
- Tall portrait crop (~240x640 ratio). Export at 2x: target height 1280 per row below.
- Clean edges - no leftover halo from the old white background.

**Page images (page-images/):**
- Format: **full-colour sRGB JPG or WebP** - keep them photographic.
- Replace the low-res/compressed source with a sharp version; many heroes render full-bleed (100vw).
- Target: **long edge 1920px** minimum (per row), keep original aspect ratio - do not re-crop.
